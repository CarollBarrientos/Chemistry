CHEMISTRY EXPERIMENT

El Roble Academy

Chemistry Lab

By Carolina Barrientos

Start Introduction and Hypothesis Materials and Procedure Results and Conclusions

Materials

- Red cabbage juice (provided by your teacher)
- Clear plastic cups or test tubes
- Small amounts of household substances:
- Lemon
- Orange juice
- Milk
- Chlorine
- Soap
- Lipton
- Yogurt
- Bicarbonate
- Coke (soda)
- Alcohol
- Pineapple juice
- Extra (pee)
- Plastic spoon or dropper
- PH Observation Sheet (to record results)

Procedure

1. Pour a small amount of cabbage juice into each cup or test tube.
2. Carefully add a small amount of a test substance to the juice.
3. Gently stir and observe the color change.
4. Record the color and decide if the substance is an acid, base, or neutral.
5. Repeat with other substances.
